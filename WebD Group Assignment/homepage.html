What We Do

Discover Our Services

Accommodations

A luxurious and comfortable hotel with top-class amenities, such as a pool, a gym with a trainer, a studio, a spa, laundry, and pick-up and drop-off services.

Event Rooms

Customers can reserve event spaces for any type of event, including weddings, birthday parties, business meetings, and more, where we also provide supplemental services like decoration, catering, photography, and music.

Bar and Dining

We also have a choice of bars and restaurants at our resort that serve both international and regional cuisine. To achieve the highest level of quality of food, our seasoned chefs employ the freshest ingredients.

Health Club

Putting our customers first, we offer a health club service where you can easily receive physio, chiropractic, rehabilitation, etc. at any time without any hassle.
